The Look E-Commerce data exploration

In this portfolio I'm analyzing TheLook E-Commerce dataset, a fictitious data created by looker studio teams The background for this project is to To Identify the performance of The look Ecommerce in the last 3 years (2019 - 2022) and identify the possible reasons of gains/loss in the last 3 years period by investigating customers and products in the E-commerce transaction data. In Summary, from analyzing the dataset, we can understand that most of the customer checking the E-commerce was not coming from organic source , and most of the customers are coming from emails ads. Other insight we can find is that in 2022, the E-commerce is having an uptrend for its sales profits for both men and women department, however, I found an abnormality in April and September where there is a gap in sales profit between man and woman department.
